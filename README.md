# Gestalt

The living ecosystem of your shared psyche.

Gestalt is an astral-ecological sandbox designed for couples. By synthesizing the birth charts of two individuals, the app generates a unique, living environment populated by "hyperentities"—digital beings that emerge, evolve, and interact based on your shared journal entries, emotional states, and interpersonal dynamics.

Think of it as a recursive laboratory for your relationship, where your internal landscapes are projected into a thriving, evolving celestial garden.

## 🌌 The Core Loop

**Birth Chart Synthesis:** Upon onboarding, the app calculates the intersection of both partners' natal charts to generate your ecosystem's "Foundational Entity"—a digital manifestation of your baseline chemistry, shared potential, and natural friction points.

**Environmental Inputs:** As you log thoughts, moods, or challenges into the shared journal, the app's AI interprets these inputs as environmental variables (e.g., temperature, light, nutrition) that alter the state of your ecosystem.

**Hyperentity Evolution:** Your entries feed the growth and behavior of entities representing your relationship.

Examples: A period of intense collaborative work might manifest as a "Construct-Spire," while unresolved conflicts might require you to tend to a "Shadow-Bloom" to prevent it from overgrowing the space.

**Collaborative Stewardship:** You and your partner do not just observe these entities; you manage them. Balancing your ecosystem requires active emotional labor, shared reflection, and the integration of your unique psychic inputs.

## 🛠 Key Features

- **Synastry Engine:** Real-time integration of current planetary transits, which act as seasonal weather patterns influencing the behavior and growth cycles of your entities.
- **Interactive Ecology:** A fluid 3D interface where you can prune, nurture, or observe the manifestations of your relationship's current state.
- **Collaborative Journaling:** A shared space that acts as the primary data feed for the system, allowing you to witness how your communication directly alters your digital world.
- **Integration Modules:** AI-driven prompts designed to help you "tame" or "integrate" challenging entities, transforming static problems into opportunities for growth.
